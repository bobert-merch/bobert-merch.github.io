The Collab

BEHIND THE DROP

Bobert

Hello fellow VDC members! My name is Bobert, I've been apart of VDC since season 5. You may know me as a past/current teammate, AGM of Party Animals, or GM of The Symphony. This season, I am playing with LG in Apprentice on team Red Rose.

Over the years, I've seen scattered interest of Franchise related merchandise, and I wanted to see if I could make that a reality for the one and only, Light Garden.

I've dabbled in the digital art realm for roughly 5 years now, but this is my first attempt at marketing it. Any and all feedback is welcome.

Light Garden

Hello everyone! Light Garden is one of the last standing franchises from the start of VDC. We have won four championships and hold the record for highest win percentage as a franchise in season 2.

We’re looking for anyone who wants to be an active, engaged member of the community and has a desire to grow. We absolutely do not tolerate any toxicity. In the end, while we’re all trying to win, this league is for fun. Our goal is to foster a friendly competitive environment where we can all learn and compete together.

Regardless of whether you end up on one our teams feel free to join our discord! We love to game together and just plain hang out, and would love to see you there with us 🙂
